Drilldown — what shows when you click an experiment?

Each panel zooms into one experiment to explain *why* it landed where it did on the cohort scatter.

A. Triple-rate timeline

qS(t), qP(t), and biomass X(t) on one chart with dual y-axis. Gray bands shade lag / exponential / stationary phases.

B. Yield trajectory + interval bars

Top: instantaneous Y_p/s(t) — when is yield best? Bottom: per-interval substrate consumed (blue) and product made (green) bars.

C. Cumulative mass dual-line

Cumulative substrate consumed and product formed on the same axis. Vertical gap at any t = local Y_p/s. Easier mental math, less kinetic detail.
