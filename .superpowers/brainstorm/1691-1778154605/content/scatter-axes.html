Cohort scatter — what go on the axes?

Each dot = one experiment. Three different framings, same underlying data.

A. Rate vs rate

x = qS_max, y = qP_max. Diagonal = every gram of carbon becomes product. Distance below diagonal = carbon going to biomass/byproducts.

B. Yield vs throughput

x = overall Y_p/s, y = qP_max. Quadrants tell you whether a condition is efficient, fast, both, or neither at a glance.

C. Cumulative mass

x = total substrate consumed, y = final product titer. Slope from origin = overall yield. No rates — just totals. Most intuitive but least kinetic.
